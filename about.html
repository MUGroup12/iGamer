About

Igamer was founded by 5 young Maynooth computer science students in December 2016.
We combined our experience to date to bring to the public.. Igamer,
an application we designed so when it comes to your gaming preferences, the work is already done.
Igamer is an application where based off a quick and easy survey for you to fill out,
we will suggest to you the best games out there on your platform so you can continue your gaming experiences
without wondering which game is the best to play! Igamer will make gaming just that bit easier for any gamer in question about any game
with such information as ratings, gaming platforms for each game and we've also included
a link to each game in case you like it! We hope that Igamer will be the the extra help you may be looking for!
